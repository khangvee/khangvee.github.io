MALAYSIA

West Malaysia

West Malaysia is made up of 11 states which are Perlis, Kedah, Penang, Perak, Selangor, Negeri Sembilan, Malacca, Johor, Pahang, Terengganu, and Kelantan.

East Malaysia

East Malaysia is made up of the two largest states, Sabah and Sarawak. It contains more tropical forests and wildlife habitat compared to West Malaysia.
